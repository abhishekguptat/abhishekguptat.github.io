Making drivers safer on the roads with mobility analytics

Context

Zendrive's platform used sensor data in mobile phones of drivers to analyze their driving behaviour. It used Accelerometer, Gyroscope, GPS, Magnetometer etc. to detect excessive speeding, aggressive acceleration, hard braking, harsh turns, risky phone use and even collisions.

This analysis was a powerful tool to derive driving risk which is used by businesses in enabling:

  • Underwriting in car insurance
  • Automatic collision detection and alert e.g. General Motor's OnStar app
  • Driving safety analytics
  • Automatic collision detection & Roadside assistance
  • Claims process
  • Customer acquisition via Insurance Qualified Leads e.g. Credit Karma app used it to display Progressive's insurance leads to its users

Zendrive is an American company its customer base mostly come from North America, LATAM and EMEA.

My Role

  • For one and half year I was the only product designer at Zendrive and worked upon Copilot app, Zendrive Web Dashboard and IQL app from ground up
  • Later another designer joined my team and I collaborated with him to build design systems, a white label app and other features utilizing Zendrive's tech
  • Duration: Aug 2019 - Feb 2022

Ecosystem analysis for Telematics/Zendrive

It was crucial to understand the bigger picture that how different players i.e. businesses which are using Zendrive tech and drivers who are end beneficiaries are deriving value from the core mission of Zendrive as 'Enabling Safe Driving'.

Copilot App

Copilot app was used to run pilots so that a customer could evaluate how different user experiences play out with Zendrive tech and how accurately and swiftly driving analysis performs.

Using BLE Beacon for tagging/identifying vehicle

In context of car insurance it is important that only the miles driven in insured car are counted for premium calculation. Similarly, small and large businesses which depend upon a fleet of vehicles need to know which vehicle they're tracking. I worked upon beacon installation flow and experience thereafter.

Smartcar car API to get/verify car info

I worked on the design of how user can connect his car with Smartcar via Copilot app which lets us get mileages drive, tyre pressure, charging status for EV etc.

Automatic collision detection and notification

Automatic collision notification (ACN) was the most sold and unique feature of Zendrive. I worked on how user can activate ACN and the experience in the moment of need when an accident has happened.

Copilot Design System

We created a robust Copilot Design System to be able to focus on solving problem at hand instead of worrying about user interface. This also made the app consistent throughout and helped engineering teams to reduce time to develop also.

User flow for Insurance Qualified Leads (IQL) app

Zendrive's IQL platform helped auto insurance companies to pitch/advertise their driving behaviour based insurance (BBI) offer to the userbase of a consumer app (publisher) which has Zendrive's tech baked into the app to measure driving behavior.

IQL app was meant for tier 2/3 insurance companies who didn't have the resources or time to launch an app on their own. I worked on the ux of this app end to end.

Dashboard for driving analytics

Dashboard enabled car fleet owners to manage and track their vehicles, drivers and their trips, driving behaviour and connectivity status.

Collision Dashboard

Collision dashboard allowed fleet owners to track collision, view details of a collision to find underlying reason and context and also use this info seamlessly in the claim process.
