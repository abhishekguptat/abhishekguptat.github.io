All-in-one communication app that makes communication safe and efficient.

iMessage-like (SMS+Chat) experience in Android

We wanted to experiment creating a single window for all the communication and activities that a user enacts in relation to another user. It included cellular calls, VoIP (WhatsApp, Viber etc.), SMS, Instant Messages, recorded calls and payments.

Chat with number hidden

Many a times we need to chat with someone but we don't want to share our phone number with the person e.g. chatting with a shop owner or an electrician etc. We wanted to give this ability to Truecaller users to be able to chat without exposing their phone numbers.

Group chat

At the time when WhatsApp groups were pivotal to chat experience of a user, it was without doubt that we wanted to cater similar experience to Truecaller chat users.

Design components for chat

I created a repo of components used in chat experience to expedite and streamline design and development process.

Smart SMS categorization & cards

Till now SMS were delivered as a condensed block of text where it was very difficult to read important info quickly. I worked upon an easily scannable cards for certain types of SMS e.g. account debit/credit, travel, ticket bookings, bills etc. which intelligently exposed relevant data points only.

Notifications, conversation and other tab for smart SMS experience

Call recording

In the mission to make communication safer, in-built call recording feature was another enabler. Many a times, scammers and spammers call us and we want to record the conversation to have an evidence in hand. Therefore, call recording experience needed to be quick, accessible and simple to understand.

Truecaller for businesses

With increasing base of Truecaller users, many of them calling from a business-related number, these business owners wanted to showcase more info about their businesses and present themselves in a trustworthy way to their customers/prospects. I worked upon how a business can create a rich profile, how its profile can reflect his business's personality and how it appears on caller ID to someone at the receving end.

Truecaller Pro & Gold

This was a growth experiment to have a premium subscription of Truecaller with no ads and a super premium subscription (called Gold) with a Golden caller ID for users who wanted to flaunt their caller ID and look special/rich.

Spam links in chat

Fake information was spreading rampantly via WhatsApp and other apps. Extending Truecaller's ability to detect spam in any form, I designed the experience how a user can report a url as spam and how he can be alerted if he receives a spam url.

'Who viewed your profile' feature

In India and Arab countries we came to know about a common nuisance specially from women that trouble makers find their number and check them on Truecaller for their profile or to call to trouble or harass. Ability to view who is checking your proifle was a really helpful in such scenarios.
